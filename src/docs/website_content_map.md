# **MIPLY Public Website Content Map & Layout Guide**

**Context for AI Web Designer/Developer:**

This document outlines the content structure and page flow for the new "MIPLY" public-facing website. MIPLY is a community and platform designed to help traditional Managed Service Providers (MSPs) evolve into category-leading **Managed Intelligence Providers (MIPs)**. The design should feel high-level, technical, strategic, and community-driven ("The Guild"). Use this map to generate page layouts, component structures, and copy placeholders.

## **1\. Global Site Architecture**

### **Site Goal**

Position MIPLY as the category leader for Managed Intelligence Providers, communicate the industry shift, demonstrate real outcomes, and invite qualified MSPs into the Guild.

### **Overall Site Journey**

Home → What Is a MIP → Guild Experience → Case Studies → Join

*(This flow moves visitors from: Industry Shift → Identity → Community → Proof → Commitment)*

### **Global Navigation (Header)**

* Home  
* What Is a MIP  
* The Guild  
* Build Log (Case Studies)  
* Join

### **Global Footer**

* **Pre-Footer Elements:** Social proof snippets, partner mentions, light "About MIPLY" section, and certifications/badges.  
* **Navigation Links:** \* Home  
  * What Is a MIP  
  * The Guild Experience  
  * Case Studies  
  * Join the Guild  
* **Action Links:** Apply, Book a Strategy Call, Watch the Overview, Contact  
* **Legal & Social:** LinkedIn, Privacy Policy, Terms of Use, Member Agreement, Login (Guild Hall participant portal)

## **2\. Page Content Outlines**

### **Page 1: Home — Enter the Guild**

**Purpose:** High-level positioning, identity shift, outcome promise, and primary call to join.

**Primary Outcome:** Visitor understands the shift and feels invited into a high-level technical community.

**Key Content Blocks / Layout Needs:**

* **Hero Section:** "From MSP to MIP. Welcome to the Guild."  
* **Industry Shift:** Why traditional MSP services are no longer enough.  
* **What a MIP Delivers:** Outcome-focused intelligence services.  
* **What Is MIPLY:** Community \+ roadmap \+ capability building.  
* **Life Inside the Guild:** Peer collaboration and applied learning.  
* **Outcomes in Action:** Teaser case studies with measurable results.  
* **Future-Focused Close \+ CTA:** Apply to Join the Guild.

### **Page 2: What Is a MIP? — The Category Page**

**Purpose:** Educate the market and establish MIPLY as the authority defining the Managed Intelligence Provider model.

**Primary Outcome:** Visitor understands the identity shift and sees MIP as the future-facing model.

**Key Content Topics / Layout Needs:**

* **The Problem:** The limits of the traditional MSP model.  
* **The Solution:** The rise of intelligence-driven service models.  
* **Definition:** What a MIP actually does (through a technical \+ strategic lens).  
* **The 5 Outcome Lenses (Ideal for a Grid/Card Layout):**  
  1. Faster operations  
  2. Lower costs  
  3. Better employee experience  
  4. Better client experience  
  5. Greater operational visibility  
* **The Tech:** How MIPs use Data, Automation, and AI together.  
* **Urgency:** Why this shift matters now.  
* *Note for Copy/Design:* Include subtle positioning language around capability transfer and client-safe architectures.

### **Page 3: The Guild Experience**

**Purpose:** Show what participation feels like and how members build real capability.

**Primary Outcome:** Visitor sees Guild membership as applied, collaborative, and outcome-driven.

**Key Content Topics / Layout Needs:**

* **What the Guild Is:** A technical and strategic community for builders, not spectators.  
* **How the Guild Works (Process/Timeline Layout):**  
  * Biweekly peer groups (Sales and Operations)  
  * Shared frameworks and implementation patterns  
  * Build Lab and co-development pathways  
  * Continuous skill development  
* **What Members Build (Deliverables/Features Layout):**  
  * Intelligence-driven service offerings  
  * Repeatable delivery models  
  * Internal technical capability  
* **Who Thrives in the Guild:** Growth-oriented MSP leaders and teams ready to evolve their service model.

### **Page 4: MIPs in Action — Case Studies (Build Log)**

**Purpose:** Provide proof through real-world, outcome-driven examples (Blog/Feed format).

**Primary Outcome:** Visitor sees measurable business impact and recognizes the model is repeatable.

**Key Content Topics / Layout Needs:**

* **Case Study 1: MSP to MIP Transformation**  
  * Identity shift & Business alignment  
  * Intelligence services introduced  
* **Case Study 2: Copilot Agent Legal Use Case**  
  * Workflow transformation & Reduced onboarding time  
  * Labor optimization & Increased win rates  
* **Standard Case Study Template Structure:**  
  * Starting challenge  
  * What was built or implemented  
  * Measurable outcomes  
  * Quote from leadership  
  * Callout on transferable patterns for other industries

### **Page 5: Join the Guild**

**Purpose:** Convert interest into action and filter for the right members.

**Primary Outcome:** Qualified visitors feel motivated and confident taking the next step.

**Key Content Topics / Layout Needs:**

* **Who MIPLY Is For:**  
  * MSPs feeling commoditized  
  * Leaders seeking higher-value services  
  * Teams ready to build intelligence capability  
* **What Participation Looks Like (Expectations):**  
  * Engagement expectations & Collaboration model  
  * Commitment to implementation  
* **What Members Gain (Benefits/Value Prop):**  
  * Stronger client relationships & New revenue pathways  
  * Higher strategic relevance & Internal capability that compounds over time  
* **Primary CTA:** Application or Strategy Call booking form/link.  
* **FAQ Section (Accordion Layout):**  
  * *Do we need AI expertise already?*  
  * *Are we too small?*  
  * *How much time is required?*
